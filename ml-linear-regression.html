Problem

How can we estimate the price of a souvlaki wrap based on the price of a single souvlaki stick?

Goal

Build a simple AI model that predicts wrap prices.

Linear Regression

How the Prediction Works

Linear regression finds a straight-line relationship between two things. In this project, it connects stick price and wrap price.

This means as the stick price increases, the wrap price increases too. The value α represents the extra cost of pita, ingredients, and profit.

Wrap ≈ α × Stick

How We Calculate Alpha (α)

We calculate alpha by averaging the values from all shops:

α = (sum of all values) ÷ number of shops

Evaluation

Why This Works — And Where It Simplifies

Benefits

Simple model, real-world context, quick interaction, and a strong example of how linear regression can explain everyday prices.

Limitations

Prices vary by ingredients, portion size, neighborhood, labor costs, and brand positioning, so the model remains intentionally simplified.
